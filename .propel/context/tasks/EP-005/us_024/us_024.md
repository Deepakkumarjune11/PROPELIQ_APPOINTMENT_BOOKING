# User Story - US_024

## Story ID
* ID: US_024

## Story Title
* Role-Based Authentication & Session Management

## Description
* As a platform user, I want role-based authentication with strict separation between patient, staff, and admin capabilities and automatic session timeout, so that I can only access features appropriate to my role and PHI is protected from unauthorized access.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Given** I am an unauthenticated user, **When** I access a protected resource, **Then** the system redirects me to the login page (SCR-024) per FR-001.

2. **Given** I provide valid credentials, **When** I log in, **Then** the system authenticates me via ASP.NET Core Identity and establishes a session with my role (patient, staff, or admin) per TR-010.

3. **Given** I am authenticated as a patient, **When** I attempt to access staff or admin features, **Then** the system returns 403 Forbidden and the UI hides unauthorized navigation items per NFR-004.

4. **Given** I am authenticated, **When** I am inactive for 15 minutes, **Then** the session terminates and I must re-authenticate per FR-017 and NFR-005.

5. **Given** I am active in my session, **When** 14 minutes of inactivity pass, **Then** a modal warning appears: "Your session will expire in 1 minute. Stay logged in?" with "Yes" and "Logout" buttons.

## Edge Cases
* What happens when a session timeout occurs during form submission? (System queues the submission, redirects to login, and replays after re-authentication if possible)
* How does the system handle simultaneous logins from different devices? (Each session is independent; timeout applies per session)
