# User Story - US_022

## Story ID
* ID: US_022

## Story Title
* Conflict Detection & Resolution

## Description
* As a staff member, I want the system to detect clinically meaningful conflicts across aggregated patient data and require my explicit resolution before the patient summary is verified, so that contradictory information is reviewed and corrected by a human before clinical use.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Given** the 360-degree view is assembled, **When** contradictory facts are detected (e.g., different medications, conflicting history), **Then** the system flags them as conflict items with a red conflict badge and count per FR-013.

2. **Given** conflicts are flagged, **When** I review a conflict item, **Then** I see all conflicting values from their respective source documents displayed side-by-side per AIR-004.

3. **Given** I am resolving a conflict, **When** I select the correct value or enter a manual override, **Then** the system updates the consolidated_facts, clears the conflict flag, and records my justification in the audit log per FR-013.

4. **Given** unresolved conflicts exist, **When** I attempt to mark the patient summary as verified, **Then** the system blocks verification until all conflicts are explicitly acknowledged or resolved.

## Edge Cases
* What happens when staff rejects a conflict resolution candidate? (Staff enters a manual override with mandatory audit justification per UC-005 extension 3a)
* How does the system handle conflicts across 10+ sources? (System groups conflicts by fact type, shows a summary table, and allows batch resolution with individual justification)
