# User Story - US_012

## Story ID
* ID: US_012

## Story Title
* Conversational AI Intake

## Description
* As a patient, I want to complete intake through an AI-assisted conversational interface, so that I can provide my clinical information naturally through guided questions without navigating complex forms.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Given** I choose conversational intake mode, **When** the chat interface loads, **Then** the AI assistant greets me and asks the first intake question with clear, empathetic language.

2. **Given** I am in the conversational flow, **When** I provide an answer, **Then** the AI processes my response within 3 seconds at p95 and asks the next relevant question per AIR-002.

3. **Given** I want to switch modes, **When** I click "Switch to manual form", **Then** the system transitions to the manual form with all conversationally-gathered answers pre-populated per FR-003.

4. **Given** the conversational intake is complete, **When** all required information has been gathered, **Then** the system stores the intake response with mode="conversational" and structured answers as JSONB.

5. **Given** the AI service is unavailable, **When** I attempt conversational intake, **Then** the system falls back to the manual form with a message explaining the AI assistant is temporarily unavailable.

## Edge Cases
* What happens when the patient provides ambiguous or off-topic responses? (AI re-prompts with a more specific question and offers to switch to manual mode)
* How does the system handle conversation timeout? (After 5 minutes of inactivity, session is preserved and patient can resume or switch to manual)
