# User Story - US_011

## Story ID
* ID: US_011

## Story Title
* Manual Intake Form

## Description
* As a patient, I want to complete intake through a manual form with field-by-field entry, so that I can provide required clinical information at my own pace with full control over my answers.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Given** I choose the manual intake option, **When** the form loads, **Then** I see all required intake questions displayed as form fields with progress indication.

2. **Given** I am completing the manual form, **When** I switch to conversational AI mode, **Then** my previously entered manual answers are preserved and available in the conversational context per FR-003.

3. **Given** I have partially completed the form, **When** I return to the manual form from conversational mode, **Then** all previously entered answers (from both modes) are pre-populated in the form fields.

4. **Given** I submit the completed intake form, **When** the submission is processed, **Then** the system stores the intake response with mode="manual" and all answers as JSONB payload.

## Edge Cases
* What happens when the patient navigates away mid-form? (Partial answers are preserved in local state; returning to the page restores progress)
* How does the system handle very long free-text answers? (Text fields enforce character limits with visible counter; overflow is truncated with warning)
